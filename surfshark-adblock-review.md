## Key Features

CleanWeb blocks banner ads, video ads, and pop-ups on most websites. Pages load faster since less content needs to download. Particularly noticeable on news sites and video platforms.

CleanWeb checks URLs before loading and blocks known malicious sites, malware-hosting domains, and phishing resources. Particularly relevant for Ukrainian users where phishing attacks targeting bank customers are common.
