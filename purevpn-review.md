## Security & Privacy

**No-logs audit:** KPMG conducted an independent no-logs audit — giving PureVPN more credibility than unaudited competitors.

**Historical incident:** In 2017, PureVPN provided connection metadata to US authorities that helped identify a cyberstalker. The company argues it only shared connection metadata (not browsing activity) in a criminal case — distinguishing it from a full logs disclosure. The incident highlighted that "no-logs" policies vary in scope.

**Jurisdiction:** British Virgin Islands — outside US/EU jurisdiction. Better privacy protection than US-based VPNs.
