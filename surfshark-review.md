## Security Features

### Camouflage Mode
Automatically activates when using OpenVPN and disguises VPN traffic as regular HTTPS traffic. Unlike NordVPN's obfuscated servers (which require manual selection), Camouflage Mode enables itself when needed.

**For Ukrainian users:** Useful on corporate networks or ISPs that throttle VPN traffic. Traffic appears as standard web browsing.

### CleanWeb (Ad/Malware Blocker)
DNS-level blocking of:
- Ads and pop-ups
- Trackers
- Malware and phishing sites
- Crypto miners

CleanWeb 2.0 (available on browser extension) adds cookie pop-up blocking and more aggressive tracker removal. Less comprehensive than NordVPN's Threat Protection but solid for everyday use.

### Bypasser (Split Tunneling)
Route specific apps or websites outside the VPN tunnel. Useful for:
- Ukrainian banking apps that block VPN connections
- Local streaming services that require Ukrainian IP
- Gaming where you want direct connection

Two modes: "Route via VPN" (only listed apps use VPN) or "Bypass VPN" (listed apps bypass VPN, everything else goes through).

### MultiHop (Double VPN)
Connect through two VPN servers for extra anonymity. Available on all paid plans. Select entry and exit countries independently.

### NoBorders Mode
Automatically activates on networks that restrict VPN usage. Selects servers optimized for bypassing restrictions — similar to NordVPN's obfuscated servers but automatic.

## Honest Downsides

**Smaller server network:** 3,200 servers vs NordVPN's 6,300+. In practice this rarely matters — Surfshark has more than enough servers in key locations. But during peak times, popular servers can get slightly more congested.

**No Meshnet equivalent:** NordVPN's Meshnet (private encrypted network between devices) has no Surfshark equivalent. For teams needing device-to-device networking, NordVPN wins.

**Weaker Threat Protection:** CleanWeb is good but NordVPN's Threat Protection is more comprehensive — particularly its standalone malware blocking that works without VPN active.

**Netherlands jurisdiction:** Surfshark is incorporated in the Netherlands (EU) — slightly less privacy-favorable than Panama (NordVPN) or Switzerland (ProtonVPN). No-logs audit by Deloitte compensates.
