# About VPN Ukraine Hub

## Who I Am

My name is **Andrii Hnitko**. I'm a developer and digital researcher from Poltava, Ukraine.

Since 2022, VPNs have become essential tools for Ukrainian internet users — for privacy, security, and bypassing restrictions. I couldn't find a single independent, Ukrainian-language VPN review site written by an actual user, so I built one.

## What This Site Is

**VPN Ukraine Hub** is an independent review platform covering VPN services for Ukrainian users. Every review is researched and written by me personally — not auto-generated, not paid by VPN vendors.

- 33 articles in Ukrainian and English
- Real pricing tested from Ukrainian payment methods (PrivatBank, Monobank)
- Focus on what matters for Ukraine: censorship bypass, privacy, speed to EU servers

## Transparency

Some links on this site are affiliate links. If you purchase through them, I earn a small commission — at no extra cost to you. This helps keep the site running. I only recommend services I've personally researched.

Affiliate partners: NordVPN, ProtonVPN, PureVPN, IPVanish, HideMyName, NordPass.
