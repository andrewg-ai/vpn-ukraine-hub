## Security & Privacy

**No-logs controversy:** In 2016, IPVanish (under previous ownership) provided user logs to US authorities despite claiming a no-logs policy. This is a significant historical red flag. Under Ziff Davis ownership since 2019, IPVanish has updated policies, but the no-logs claim has not been independently audited.

**For Ukrainian users:** IPVanish is adequate for everyday privacy — encrypting traffic on public WiFi, bypassing geo-restrictions. Not recommended for high-risk privacy use cases given the historical logs incident and US jurisdiction.

**Encryption:** AES-256 on all protocols. Standard industry protection.
