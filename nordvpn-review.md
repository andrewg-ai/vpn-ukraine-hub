### No-Logs Policy
NordVPN's no-logs policy has been independently verified by PricewaterhouseCoopers in both 2022 and 2023. The policy was also validated in practice — in 2018 a NordVPN server in Finland was seized by authorities. No user data was recovered because none existed.

### Threat Protection
NordVPN's most underrated feature. Threat Protection:
- Blocks malware, spyware, and phishing sites at DNS level
- Removes trackers and intrusive ads from web pages
- Scans downloaded files for malware
- **Works even when VPN is disconnected** — functions as standalone security software

This means NordVPN provides malware protection 24/7, not just when tunneling traffic.

### Double VPN
Routes traffic through two VPN servers sequentially. First server sees your real IP but not destination. Second server sees destination but only first server's IP. Useful for journalists, activists, or anyone needing maximum anonymity.

### Obfuscated Servers
Special servers that disguise VPN traffic as regular HTTPS. Useful in environments that block VPN protocols — corporate networks, some ISPs, or countries with VPN restrictions.

### Meshnet
Create private encrypted networks between your own devices or invite others. Up to 60 devices per network. Use cases:
- Access home computer from anywhere as if on local network
- Small team collaboration without corporate VPN infrastructure
- Gaming LAN parties over internet
- Available free even without NordVPN subscription
